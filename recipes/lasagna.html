Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish featuring wide, flat pasta sheets layered with rich fillings like tomato sauce (ragù), creamy béchamel or ricotta, cheeses (mozzarella, Parmesan), and often ground meat or vegetables, then baked until bubbly and golden, representing comfort food with diverse regional variations.

  1. 2 olive oil. plus extra for the dish.
  2. 750g lean beef mince.
  3. 90g pack prosciutto.
  4. 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce.
  5. 200ml hot beef stock.
  6. nutmeg.
  7. 300g fresh lasagne sheets.
